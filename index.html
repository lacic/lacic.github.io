Emanuel Lacić is a Software Engineer and Scientific Researcher in the Social Computing division at the Know-Center. Additionally, he is a PHD student at the Knowledge Technologies Institute of Graz University of Technology. He has a M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Software Engineering and Information Systems from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing at the University of Zagreb. He is currently doing his PhD at Graz University of Technology in the course of the European-funded project Learning Layers on real-time recommender systems in large-scale settings. Apart from that, his research is utilized in several industry and European-funded projects such as AFEL or MoreGrasp. His main research interests are in the fields of Recommender Systems, Deep Learning, Information Retrieval, Big Data, as well as Social and Complex Network Analysis.
